Avatar, Net Art, Javascript p5.js

(Click on "Draw Frog"!!)

This piece serves as a representation of my internet personality My avatar represents the digital me because I put stuff that I like into it. Recently, I’ve had a weird obsession with cutely drawn frogs on the internet. I also have a frog plushie that is also a heater. In regards to the pastel color choices, I wear a lot of pastel and they’re my favorite colors because of how calming and cute it is. I decided to add a laser and crosshair one because my friend suggested the lasers and two because I recently started playing computer games that are shooting games. Overall, I just wanted to create a cute little sketch that can also be played with!
