"This Land is Our Land", Digital Video, Three Minutes

(WARNING! This video can be sensitive to viewers due to the videos that deplict real events that happened regarding shootings.)

A cultural critique on the gun control laws of The United States of America. America's famous folk song "This Land is Your Land" by Woody Guthrie is sung to represent the seemingly utopian land of America that actually gives no ownership to property and the racist people in the government and in power.

"Move!", Digital Video, Two Minutes

This piece explores things that people, including me, use to decrease their anxiety. It is a space for the audience to relax from their everyday troubles. The title of this piece is both a form of irony and what you should do next. The space should be a place for relaxation, but also a reminder to keep yourself busy and move on from the past or whatever is making you anxious.
