 Art-75: Introduction to Digital Video 

This class explored the digital space of video art in the context of digital media art and web design.

"Paris", Digital Video, One Minute, 2019

A short compilation of videos introducing the beauty of Paris. This project focuses more on the timing of the video clips to the music.

"A Short Love Story", Digital Video, One Minute, 2019

An experiment with camera angles in a short film involving love between two people with an additional individual trying to ruin their relationship.

"This Land is Our Land", Digital Video, Three Minutes, 2019

(WARNING! This video can be sensitive to viewers due to the videos that deplict real events that happened regarding shootings.)

A cultural critique on the gun control laws of The United States of America. America's famous folk song "This Land is Your Land" by Woody Guthrie is sung to represent the seemingly utopian land of America that actually gives no ownership to property and the racist people in the government and in power.

"Move!", Digital Video, Two Minutes, 2019

This piece explores things that people, including me, use to decrease their anxiety. It is a space for the audience to relax from their everyday troubles. The title of this piece is both a form of irony and what you should do next. The space should be a place for relaxation, but also a reminder to keep yourself busy and move on from the past or whatever is making you anxious.

"Earth", Digital Video & HTML5 & Javascript p5.js, 2020

(Click on the title of the project to view on another tab. It is best viewed that way because some Javascript code will not be seen as it is intended to be seen without visiting the webpage itself.)

This piece explores the beauty of the planet being ruined due to natural causes or manmade mistakes. There is an included two minute compitaltion video and another page with a sound clip. (Transcript included for the hearing impaired and convenience.)
