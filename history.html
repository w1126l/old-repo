Transcending Generations and Cultures, Digital Video, Three Minutes, 2018

My submission for the 2018 1990 Institute's Youth Voices on China Competition

老人们在采访中表达了对于中国政府和美国政府的看法，他们对最近的校园枪击案以及学生的安全不能得到保证的情况也表示了担忧和不满。同时，老人们也对他们的后辈寄予了很高的期望。
Two senior Chinese immigrants highlight their views on the government of China and America and America's failure to protect the students of the nation as they reflect on their own progeny and their hopes for them.
